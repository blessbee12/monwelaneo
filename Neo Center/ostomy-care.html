Ostomy Care Services

Dedicated support for individuals living with an ostomy.

Ostomy Care for Urine (Urostomy)

  • Hygiene and Health Maintenance: Prevent infections and maintain skin integrity around the stoma.
  • Urine Collection: Ensure proper usage of urostomy pouches for hygiene and leak prevention.
  • Quality of Life: Support daily activities with minimal disruption and provide emotional support.
  • Complication Prevention: Monitor for issues like stoma changes, blockages, or infections.
  • Education and Independence: Train patients and caregivers in pouch management and stoma care.
